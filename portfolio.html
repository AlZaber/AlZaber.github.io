Shader Based - Smooth Bezier Curve Rendering

Our research is shader based Cubic and Quadratic Bezier curve rendering technique developed by Loop-Blinn, which requires at least two triangles to render a cubic curve and one triangle for quadratic curve, reducing total vertex count.
No tessellation needed to approximate boundary of the curve, producing buttery smooth curves and which is suitable for rendering in low end devices.
